Stay Safe Online Checklist

General Safety Tips
- Change your passwords regularly and ensure they are unique and strong  
- Enable 2-factor authentication  
- Do not disclose your personal information to unknown sites  
- Do not click on suspicious emails and unknown attachments  
- Only use official apps and sites to perform transactions  
- Ensure your devices are updated with the latest security patches  
- Always verify the authenticity of unknown callers and messages  
- Always check your bank and app transaction history

Financial / UPI Safety Tips
- Never disclose your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank account information to anyone  
- Remember: Your UPI PIN is only for sending money, not receiving money!  
- Never allow any unknown or unexpected payment requests  
- Never scan any random QR code sent by an unknown person 

Reporting Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930
